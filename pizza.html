Pizza:

Pizza is another beloved and widely enjoyed food. It originated in Naples, Italy, and has since become a global culinary phenomenon. The basic components of pizza include a thin or thick crust, tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings. The toppings can range from meats like pepperoni and sausage to vegetables like mushrooms, bell peppers, and olives.

Here are some common types of pizza.
1.Margherita: A classic pizza with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, basil, and olive oil.
2.Pepperoni: Topped with pepperoni, a type of cured sausage.
3.Vegetarian: Loaded with various vegetables like bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, and sometimes tomatoes.
4.Hawaiian: Featuring ham and pineapple as key toppings.
5.Meat Lovers: Packed with a variety of meats such as sausage, pepperoni, bacon, and sometimes ham or ground beef.
6.BBQ Chicken: Topped with barbecue sauce, chicken, red onions, and sometimes cilantro.
