Rolls:

1.Bread Rolls: These are typically small, round bread portions that can be served as a side or used as a sandwich base.
2.Dinner Rolls: Soft, fluffy, and often served warm, dinner rolls are a common accompaniment to meals.
3.Sausage Rolls: A savory pastry that encases seasoned sausage meat, often baked until golden brown.
4.Cinnamon Rolls: Sweet rolls made with a cinnamon and sugar filling, usually topped with icing or glaze.
5.Egg Rolls: As mentioned earlier, these are a type of Chinese-American dish where a wheat-flour wrapper is filled with a mixture of chopped vegetables and often meat, then deep-fried.
