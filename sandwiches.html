Sandwiches:

Sandwiches are a versatile and popular type of food enjoyed worldwide. A basic sandwich typically consists of two slices of bread with some type of filling in between. The filling can include a variety of ingredients such as:

1.Cold Cuts: Sliced meats like turkey, ham, roast beef, or chicken.
2.Cheese: Various types of cheese can be used, such as cheddar, Swiss, provolone, or mozzarella.
3.Vegetables: Lettuce, tomatoes, onions, cucumbers, bell peppers, and more can add freshness and crunch.
4.Condiments: Mayonnaise, mustard, ketchup, and various spreads can enhance the flavor.
5.Spreads: Hummus, pesto, or various sauces can be used to add moisture and flavor.
6.Extras: Bacon, avocado, pickles, and other ingredients can be added for extra taste and texture.
